Summer River Mass

  • Buffalo RiverFest Park249 Ohio Street, Buffalo, NY 14204
    Mass is held at the Pavilion or the Pergola — see this week’s spot at the top of the page.
  • Sundays at 9:00 AMJune 28 through August 23 (weather permitting)
  • ParkingStreet parking is available, or use the grassy-knoll lots across the street. Please do not park in the paved lot next to the lodge.
  • All are welcome!Families, children, and well-behaved dogs too.
  • Some seating providedA limited number of chairs are available — you’re warmly welcome to bring a lawn chair or blanket of your own.
  • Donuts after MassFr. Betti always brings Paula’s Donuts, plus water and juice boxes for the kids — and the grown-ups, too.

Campus Mass

  • Canisius University Campus2001 Main Street, Buffalo, NY 14208
    Held outdoors on campus — moving into Christ the King Chapel if the weather doesn’t cooperate.
  • Sundays at 7:00 PMOutdoors, weather permitting.
  • All are welcome!Students, families, and neighbors.
